1️⃣2️⃣ Tone & Typography Guidance (for UI team)

Headings:
	•	Sentence case
	•	No exclamation marks
	•	No emotive verbs

Numbers:
	•	Large
	•	High contrast
	•	Primary focus

Context text:
	•	Muted grey
	•	Smaller size
	•	Calm spacing

No red warning tones unless capital reaches zero.
